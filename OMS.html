    About OMS
    Welcome to the Orchid Math Society, where passion meets precision in the world of mathematics. Founded with the vision of fostering a community of math enthusiasts, our society is dedicated to exploring the beauty and complexity of mathematics in all its forms. At Orchid Math Society, we believe that mathematics is not just a subject but an adventure waiting to be explored. Whether you're a seasoned mathematician or someone who simply enjoys the occasional equation, our society offers a welcoming space to learn, discuss, and appreciate the wonders of mathematics.

    Inter School Maths Hunt
    Get ready for an exhilarating journey of logic, problem-solving, and teamwork! The Inter-College Math Hunt brings together the brightest minds from colleges across the region to compete in an exciting and challenging math-based treasure hunt.

    What to Expect?

    Creative Puzzles & Challenges: Solve riddles, equations, and brain-teasers designed to test your math skills and critical thinking.

    Teamwork & Collaboration: Form teams with fellow math enthusiasts and collaborate to uncover clues and complete challenges.

    Prizes & Glory: Stand a chance to win amazing prizes and earn bragging rights as the ultimate math champions!

    Date : 2024-11-30

    Venue : Orchid College

    Registration Deadline: 2024-11-29 (for registration form and any inqury related to event contact us at orchidmathsociety@gmail.com)

    Eligibility: Open to all students from participating colleges.

    Prepare to put your math skills to the test and embark on an unforgettable adventure. Whether you're a math prodigy or a puzzle enthusiast, there's something for everyone!

    Join us and let the hunt begin!

    Intra School Maths Mystique
    The Intra-School Maths Mystique, organized by OMS, brings together some of the brightest mathematical minds for a challenging competition that promises a blend of intellectual challenges, excitement, and exceptional problem-solving. This prestigious event was held at the Orchid College Library on August 9, 2024, offering a platform for students to showcase their mathematical acumen and compete in a spirit of healthy rivalry.
